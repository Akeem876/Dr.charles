steps you can take

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 30 seconds. If soap and water are not available, use an alcohol- based hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol. Avoiding touching your face, nose, eyes or mouth with unwashed hands. Avoiding close contact with people who are sick. Cover coughs and sneezes with a tissue. Then throw the tissue away and wash your hands. Stay home when sick We are here to support your dental and overall health.
