meet dr. michelle charles

Dr. Michelle J. Charles graduated from Howard University College of Dentistry in 1995 and from a general practice in dentistry residency program in Miami / Jackson Memorial Hospital in 1996. She has been restoring healthy smiles at her private practice in Orlando Florida since 1996. With over 20 years experience, she has taken extensive post-graduate training in general dentistry and in Botox and fillers. She and her staff deliver personalized one-to-one patient care, using the latest advancements that modern dentistry has to offer.

You can expect personal, one-on-one attention from our entire team. At Dr. Michelle Charles Dental Associates, in Kingston, Jamaica, we understand that each of our patients has unique wants and needs. That’s why we promote open communication, to understand what you are looking for in your smile, so we can design a care plan that is right for you. We take the time to explain your options, listen to your concerns and answer any questions you may have.
